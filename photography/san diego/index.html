San Diego
Fujifilm X-T2, La Jolla, 2024
A day trip to San Diego.
Seals, sea lions, and people at the beach.
I usually struggle with taking photos in daylight because of the excessive brightness, and it was such a sunny day that day.
Two different takes of the post that I found along the shore. I wanted to show how lonely yet calming it looks when devoid of people. The pigeon was a paid actor.
I thought of making the railings look like a frame to capture the poised pigeon standing below. As the sun was going down and it was getting cold, I thought the dark tone really suits the atmostphere in that moment.
On the way home, the sunset on the west side looked beautiful. However, I was driving and could not take a proper photo of it. With some (dangerous) attempts to snap photos without looking at the camera, this one was the least blurry. What really made it stand out was the car in the side mirror. That contrast between the warm headlights and the cool blue sky from behind.
