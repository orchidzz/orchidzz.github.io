Aquarium 2
Fujifilm X-T2, Shedd Aquarium, 2024
Another aquarium photoshoot in another city.
This pool of penguins looked magical in person thanks to the darkness of the exhibit's hallway and the dreamy light within the pool. The window screen surrounding the exhibit was dirty but it added a misty effect to the photo as the light hits the swimming penguin.
I tried to experiment with shutter speeds to capture blurry photos of these creatures in motion in focus while the background is blurried, but these were the best that I can do. Fuji's rendition of green is also so nice.
Underwater Plant.

I was surprised by how clear it came out, looking as if there's no water or a screen in front at all. I loved the magenta ligthing in the tank as well, which contrasts well with the green.
Swimming in the Dark.

There's actually a reflection of the fish on top, which was my original idea when capturing this photo--a swimming fish and its reflection. However, the raw background or negative space on top was much brighter than it is now, and darkening it in post-processing using masks did not work well for the reflection subject. Thus, I decided to remove the reflection altogether. In contrast with the background, the white, flowy body and fins of the fish make it seem dreamy.
