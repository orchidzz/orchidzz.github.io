Chicago
Fujifilm X-T2, Downtown, Summer 2024
I got the chance to go to Chicago during the summer for an internship and these photos were taken on the same day as I explored downtown.
The famous bean. I was rushing to take the photo because there were so many people, but it was a coincidence that I was able to capture the buildings behind. The architecture there was beautiful.
Various colorful spots I found.
The long trail along the river was beautiful. I thought the blues complement well with the red cones and warning signs along the way.
My attempt at black and white photos. The parking structure really caught my eyes as I saw it from the Riverwalk. It's a shame my lens couldn't capture the whole building.
